======= Music Player

Information:

This is a simple player made ​​with HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript that started with a simple study of the audio API. The biggest difficulty is to always have a version of the song for each browser type, for it was the idea of ​​integrating everything with Deezer and Youtube.

New features like lyrics and buy music on iTunes soon.
